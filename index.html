      How to play

      Select a Frenzy you would like to play

      Select your difficulty, Higher difficulties provide extra points

      After clicking "Play Frenzy", Time will start counting down, in which you will have unlimited guesses

      Each round is weighted to start with an easier word and gradually increase in difficulty

      You need to guess each word(s) correctly, if the timer reaches 00, you will fail

      Hints are given as time progresses depending on the amount of time you're given

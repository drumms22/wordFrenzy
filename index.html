        How to play

        Modes

        Each mode offers a unique challenge

        Currently, there is one avalable mode (two more on the way!)

        Frenzy

        Word are random and ordered shortest to longest

        You can complete a maximum of 5 words per Frenzy

        Words are individually timed

        Time counts down

        If time reaches zero, you fail the challenge

        Head To Head

        Click the "Head To Head" button in the menu

        You will have 2 options, creating a lobby and viewing invites

        If you have invites availble, you will be able to join or deny an invite

        Rather be a lobby leader? Create your own and invite friends

        Lobby leaders can invite players and change lobby settings

        Once the lobby is full, the leader can start the game

        Head To Head works similarly to Single player, other than a few differences

        All players are given the same time per word and hints

        Hints are maximum 2 per word and can be used whenever needed

        Refreshing the browser will auto kick you from the game after it has started

        Once the Head To Head match is complete, all players are put back into the lobby

        Stats

        Total Points

        Points are accumulated by completing words, challenges, and other sources

        Word points are valued based on scores (shown below)

        • 1 Point: A, E, I, O, U, L, N, S, T, R
        • 2 Points: D, G
        • 3 Points: B, C, M, P
        • 4 Points: F, H, V, W, Y
        • 5 Points: K
        • 8 Points: J, X
        • 10 Points: Q, Z

        The cap for total points is currently unlimited but may be capped in the future

        Total Rank

        Ranks are numerical achievements from 0-100

        Each rank is 100 points

        More ranks and achievements will be added in the future

        Total Time

        Total time is the amount of time you spend in activites

        Total Words

        Total words is the total amount words you have completed

        Total Challenges

        Total challenges is the total amount challenges you have completed

        Current Speed

        Current speed represents how fast you guess words

        The time given during single player activites is based on this value

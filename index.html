Word Frenzy

How to play

You will be attempting a Frenzy Challenge

You get 3 words starting from 4 characters to 6 characters in length

Time is based off of "seconds per character (spc)": sweeps is 20spc * 5char = 100 seconds

Time will start counting down, in which you will have unlimited guesses

You will progress through the words in ascending order shortest to longest

You need to guess each word correctly, if the timer gets to 00, you will fail

Successful completion of the challenge rewards an extra 15 points

**Please remember, this is for TESTING, not what will be in the final product!
